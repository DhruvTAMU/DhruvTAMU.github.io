Society of Sonic Flight Engineers

SSFE is an Aggie-run organization dedicated to develop aircraft to set new airspeed records. Through a collaborative design process, members learn to apply concepts in aerodynamics, structural analysis, and avionics in pursuit of sonic flight.

SSFE Engineers are currently finishing Design Cycle 1, which will culminate in a Fly Day where members can finally see the rewards of the dedicated design and build process.
