Design 1 - The Miles Mk.1

Afternoon, April 27th, in the Big 2025. A gentle breeze offered relief from the blistering sun that was gradually revealing itself as the clouds dissipated. SSFE’s caravan of cars and trucks pulled up one by one at the scene: The runway at Rellis Airfield, which once hosted the US Air Force. Today, it would witness SSFE’s inaugural flight of the aircraft named Miles Mk.1.

SSFE’s first ever radio-controlled aircraft, the Miles Mk. 1, took off from the Rellis Airfield Runway in Bryan, TX at 5:00 PM CST. The Mk. 1 flew two racetrack-style laps performing 4 banked turns 80 ft away from the ground pilot, accelerating through the straightaways in between.

After two laps, the aircraft successfully touched down on the pavement. Our onboard GPS system coupled with video analysis determined the aircraft’s top speed during a straightaway acceleration was 72 mph airspeed or 82 mph groundspeed.
